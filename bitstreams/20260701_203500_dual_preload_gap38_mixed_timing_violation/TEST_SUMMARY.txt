Interpretation
--------------
Large-packet preload replay is close to the physical 100G target and the
timestamp scheduler is accurate for mixed packet sizes.  Small-packet replay is
limited by the current integer tick, one-packet-per-tick scheduler: gap=2
overdrives the link and gap=3 is stable but only about 70.4G wire estimate.

Remaining blockers
------------------
  * post-route timing is slightly negative: WNS=-0.018 ns
  * RX error counters are nonzero for large/mixed packet loopback
  * over-rate tests can stall on downstream TX tready backpressure
  * stop/clear does not yet reset the full per-port TX datapath
